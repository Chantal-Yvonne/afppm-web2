Camillah Hendricks is a seasoned Operations and Project Management professional with over a decade of experience overseeing complex, multi-stakeholder projects and organisational programmes. Camillah brings a strong blend of strategic insight and hands-on operational leadership, with extensive experience in project scheduling, quality assurance, stakeholder engagement, and workforce-related operations that support sustainable, scalable impact across AFPPM's initiatives.
