CodeGirls Cape Town

CodeGirls SA is a pioneering initiative dedicated to bridging the gender gap in the technology industry. Their mission is to empower young women by providing them with high-quality training in software development, business skills, and career readiness. Through mentorship, hands-on projects, and industry partnerships, they create opportunities for young women to thrive in the tech sector. Their programme is designed to equip participants with the skills, confidence, and network needed to secure sustainable employment in the digital economy. While CodeGirls have already empowered over 1800 girls in Pakistan, 70% of their graduates are now thriving in the tech sector. CodeGirls replicated and expanded this success in South Africa, where there is a significant need for gender equity in IT.
