Womandla

Womandla Reskilling Revolution Africa (WRRA) is part of the Reskilling Revolution Africa (RRA) initiative, a skills and employability programme delivered by Womandla Foundation in partnership with International Association for Volunteer Effort (IAVE) and IBM SkillsBuild. It focuses on equipping youth and women with future‐focused digital and work skills to improve their economic opportunities. AFPPM will be deliver the programme to 1500 unique learners locally over a 5-month period.
